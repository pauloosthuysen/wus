Our Vision for Wish Upon A Star Industries is to build a company, focused on five star quality service and ongoing customer support in each and every field we continue to grow in.

We are all about people. Our mission is to build a sustainable workforce, empowering people, and regaining much needed skills, dedicated to customer satisfaction and quality end results.

This entails employing passionate, qualified & talented people in positions of responsibility, who in turn can teach and train others.

Our Mission is to not limit ourselves in order to achieve a successful business with the main aspect being development of skills, employment, ensuring service ethics that we embrace and that in turn will rebuild and serve South Africa, our Country.

Our current field of expertise consists of Project Managers, Forensic experts, Furniture and decor consultants, Building contractors, maintenance teams, qualified engineers, procurement specialists, recruitment specialists, manufactures, to name but a few.

It means that our customers get the answers they need from people who are passionate about what they do.

When people are empowered to do the right thing, customers develop a lasting memory. That’s what we strive for everyday, with people that are extraordinary—that’s the Wish Upon A Star way.
