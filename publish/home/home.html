About Us

We are all about people. Our mission is to build a sustainable workforce, empowering people, and regaining much needed skills, dedicated to customer satisfaction and quality end results.

This entails employing passionate, qualified & talented people in positions of responsibility, who in turn can teach and train others. Our Mission is to not limit ourselves in order to achieve a successful business with the main aspect being development of skills, employment, ensuring service ethics that we embrace and that in turn will rebuild and serve South Africa, our Country.

When people are empowered to do the right thing, customers develop a lasting memory. That’s what we strive for everyday, with people that are extraordinary—that’s the Wish Upon A Star way.
